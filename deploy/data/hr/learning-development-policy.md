# Learning & Professional Development Policy

**Policy ID:** HR-POL-008  
**Effective Date:** April 1, 2025  
**Last Revised:** September 30, 2025  
**Approved By:** Sarah Mitchell, VP of Human Resources

---

## 1. Purpose

This policy outlines the professional development benefits and expectations for all employees at Contoso Ltd., reinforcing the company's commitment to continuous learning.

## 2. Learning & Development Budget

Each full-time employee is entitled to an annual **Learning & Development (L&D) budget** based on their job level:

| Level   | Annual L&D Budget (CAD) |
| ------- | ----------------------- |
| 1–3     | $1,500                  |
| 4–5     | $2,500                  |
| 6–7     | $3,500                  |
| 8+      | $5,000                  |

### 2.1 Eligible Expenses

The L&D budget may be used for:
- Online courses, MOOCs, and e-learning platforms (e.g., Coursera, Udemy, LinkedIn Learning)
- Professional conferences and workshops (registration fees only; travel is covered separately per HR-POL-009)
- Professional certifications and exam fees
- Technical books and publications
- Professional association memberships

### 2.2 Ineligible Expenses

- Degree programs (undergraduate or graduate) — see Section 4 for Tuition Assistance
- Hardware or equipment
- Entertainment or team social events

## 3. Learning Time

- Employees are encouraged to dedicate up to **4 hours per month** (during work hours) to professional development activities.
- Learning time does not require prior manager approval but must be logged in the **Learning Management System (LMS)**.
- Managers may temporarily restrict learning time during critical project phases with at least 1 week's notice.

## 4. Tuition Assistance Program

Employees who have completed **2 years** of continuous employment may apply for the **Tuition Assistance Program (TAP)** for accredited degree programs relevant to their current role or career path at Contoso:

| Degree Level   | Maximum Annual Reimbursement |
| -------------- | ---------------------------- |
| Bachelor's     | $5,000                       |
| Master's       | $8,000                       |
| Doctorate      | $10,000                      |

**Conditions:**
- The employee must maintain a minimum **GPA of 3.0** (or equivalent).
- Reimbursement is paid after successful completion of each semester/term upon submission of transcripts and receipts.
- If the employee leaves Contoso within **12 months** of receiving tuition assistance, the full amount for the most recent year must be repaid.

## 5. Mandatory Training

All employees must complete the following mandatory training annually:
- **Information Security Awareness** (due by March 31)
- **Workplace Harassment Prevention** (due by June 30)
- **Code of Conduct Refresher** (due by September 30)

Failure to complete mandatory training by the deadline may result in access restrictions and will be noted in the employee's performance record.

## 6. Internal Mentorship Program

Contoso Ltd. offers a voluntary **Internal Mentorship Program** matching employees with senior leaders for 6-month engagements. Applications open in January and July each year via the HR Portal.

## 7. Contact

For L&D questions, contact the **Learning & Development Team** at **learning@contoso.com** or ext. **4540**.

# Laptop & Hardware Provisioning Policy

**Policy ID:** IT-POL-002  
**Effective Date:** February 1, 2025  
**Last Revised:** July 20, 2025  
**Approved By:** James Park, Chief Information Security Officer

---

## 1. Purpose

This policy governs the provisioning, management, and return of company-issued laptops and hardware for all Contoso Ltd. employees.

## 2. Standard Equipment

All full-time employees receive the following standard equipment upon onboarding:

| Item                | Standard Specification                              |
| ------------------- | --------------------------------------------------- |
| Laptop              | Dell Latitude 5550 or MacBook Pro 14" (role-based)  |
| Monitor             | 27" Dell UltraSharp (one per employee; two for developers) |
| Keyboard & Mouse    | Wireless Microsoft Ergonomic set                    |
| Headset             | Jabra Evolve2 75 (for Teams calls)                  |
| Docking Station     | Dell WD22TB4 Thunderbolt dock                       |

### 2.1 Role-Based Upgrades

| Role Category          | Additional Equipment                                |
| ---------------------- | --------------------------------------------------- |
| Software Engineering   | 32 GB RAM minimum, second monitor                   |
| Data Science / AI      | 64 GB RAM, dedicated GPU (NVIDIA RTX)               |
| Executive (Level 10+)  | MacBook Pro 16" or equivalent, portable monitor      |
| Field Sales            | Lightweight ultrabook, mobile hotspot                |

## 3. Ordering & Provisioning

- Equipment requests are submitted via the **IT Asset Portal**.
- Standard equipment is shipped or available for pickup within **5 business days** of the request.
- Non-standard or upgraded equipment requires **manager approval** and may take up to **15 business days**.
- Equipment for new hires must be requested by HR or the hiring manager at least **10 business days** before the start date.

## 4. Hardware Refresh Cycle

| Equipment Type | Refresh Cycle |
| -------------- | ------------- |
| Laptops        | Every **3 years** |
| Monitors       | Every **5 years** |
| Peripherals    | As needed (replacement upon failure) |

- Employees approaching their refresh date will be notified **60 days** in advance.
- Early refresh may be approved for hardware failures or role changes requiring different specifications.

## 5. Care & Responsibility

Employees are responsible for:

- Keeping equipment in good working condition
- Not modifying hardware (opening cases, upgrading components) without IT approval
- Reporting damage, loss, or theft within **24 hours** to the IT Service Desk
- Not leaving equipment unattended in public places

### 5.1 Accidental Damage

- First incident: Replacement at no cost.
- Second incident within 12 months: Employee's manager is notified; additional training may be required.
- Third incident within 12 months: Cost of replacement may be partially charged to the employee's department.

### 5.2 Theft or Loss

- Must be reported to the **IT Service Desk** and **local law enforcement** within 24 hours.
- IT will remotely wipe the device immediately upon report.
- A police report number is required for a replacement to be issued.

## 6. Return of Equipment

All company equipment must be returned:

- On the employee's **last day of employment**
- When equipment is replaced during a refresh cycle
- Within **5 business days** of a role change that alters equipment entitlement

Unreturned equipment after **15 business days** from the due date will be deducted from the employee's final paycheck (where legally permitted) or referred to collections.

## 7. Personal Use

Limited personal use of company hardware is permitted provided it does not:

- Violate the Acceptable Use Policy (IT-POL-010)
- Interfere with work duties
- Introduce security risks (e.g., installing unauthorized software)

## 8. Contact

For hardware requests or issues, contact the **IT Service Desk** at **it-help@contoso.com** or ext. **5000**.

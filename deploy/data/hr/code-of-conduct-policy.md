# Code of Conduct & Workplace Behavior Policy

**Policy ID:** HR-POL-010  
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2025  
**Last Revised:** April 8, 2025  
**Approved By:** David Chen, Chief People Officer

---

## 1. Purpose

This policy establishes the standards of professional conduct expected of all employees, contractors, and interns at Contoso Ltd. It aims to foster a respectful, inclusive, and productive work environment.

## 2. Scope

This policy applies to all interactions in the course of Contoso business, whether occurring:
- On company premises
- At company-sponsored events
- In virtual meetings, chats, or emails
- On business travel
- On company social media channels

## 3. Core Expectations

All Contoso employees are expected to:

1. **Act with integrity** — Be honest, ethical, and transparent in all professional dealings.
2. **Treat others with respect** — Value diverse perspectives and communicate with courtesy.
3. **Maintain professionalism** — Represent Contoso positively in all interactions, internal and external.
4. **Protect company assets** — Use Contoso resources, data, and intellectual property responsibly.
5. **Comply with laws and policies** — Follow all applicable laws, regulations, and internal policies.

## 4. Prohibited Conduct

The following behaviors are strictly prohibited and may result in disciplinary action up to and including immediate termination:

- **Harassment:** Any unwelcome conduct based on race, gender, sexual orientation, religion, disability, age, or any other protected characteristic. This includes verbal, physical, and digital harassment.
- **Discrimination:** Making employment decisions (hiring, promotion, assignment, termination) based on protected characteristics rather than merit and qualifications.
- **Bullying:** Repeated, unreasonable behavior directed at an employee that creates a risk to health and safety, including intimidation, humiliation, or sabotage.
- **Retaliation:** Adverse action against any employee who reports a concern, files a complaint, or participates in an investigation in good faith.
- **Substance abuse:** Being under the influence of alcohol or illegal drugs during work hours or on company premises.
- **Theft or fraud:** Misappropriation of company funds, assets, or data.
- **Conflicts of interest:** Engaging in outside activities that conflict with Contoso's business interests without prior written disclosure to HR.

## 5. Reporting Concerns

Employees who witness or experience a violation of this policy are encouraged to report it through any of the following channels:

| Channel                     | Contact                          |
| --------------------------- | -------------------------------- |
| Direct Manager              | In-person or via HR Portal       |
| HR Business Partner         | hr-compliance@contoso.com        |
| Anonymous Ethics Hotline    | 1-800-555-ETHX (3849)            |
| Online Reporting            | ethics.contoso.com               |

All reports are treated **confidentially** to the extent permitted by law. Anonymous reporting is available through the Ethics Hotline and online portal.

## 6. Investigation Process

1. HR acknowledges receipt of the report within **2 business days**.
2. An investigation is initiated within **5 business days**.
3. The investigation is completed within **30 business days** (extensions may apply for complex cases).
4. Both the reporting party and the respondent are informed of the outcome in writing.
5. Investigation records are maintained for **7 years**.

## 7. Disciplinary Actions

Disciplinary measures are applied based on the severity of the violation:

| Severity   | Possible Actions                                              |
| ---------- | ------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Minor      | Verbal warning, mandatory training                            |
| Moderate   | Written warning, temporary suspension, loss of privileges     |
| Severe     | Termination of employment, legal referral                     |

## 8. Non-Retaliation Guarantee

Contoso Ltd. prohibits retaliation against any employee who reports a concern in good faith. Any employee found to have engaged in retaliation will be subject to disciplinary action, up to and including termination.

## 9. Annual Training

All employees must complete the **Workplace Conduct & Ethics** training module annually by **September 30**. Completion is tracked in the Learning Management System and factored into performance evaluations.

## 10. Contact

For conduct-related concerns, contact **hr-compliance@contoso.com** or the **Ethics Hotline** at **1-800-555-3849**.

# Travel & Expense Reimbursement Policy

**Policy ID:** HR-POL-009  
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2025  
**Last Revised:** May 15, 2025  
**Approved By:** Marie Dubois, CFO

---

## 1. Purpose

This policy governs business travel and expense reimbursement for all employees of Contoso Ltd.

## 2. Pre-Approval Requirements

- All business travel must be **pre-approved** by the employee's direct manager via the **Expense Management System (EMS)** before booking.
- International travel requires additional approval from the **department VP**.
- Travel that exceeds **$5,000 total estimated cost** requires CFO approval.

## 3. Booking Guidelines

- All flights and hotels must be booked through the company's **preferred travel agency** (Contoso Travel Portal) or the designated corporate booking tool.
- Flights: Economy class is standard. Business class is permitted for flights exceeding **6 hours** with VP approval.
- Hotels: Nightly rate must not exceed **$250 CAD** in domestic cities and **$350 CAD** in international destinations. Exceptions require manager approval.
- Rental cars: Intermediate-size or smaller. Employees must accept the rental company's insurance.

## 4. Per Diem & Meals

Daily meal allowances (per diem) for business travel:

| Meal       | Domestic (CAD) | International (CAD) |
| ---------- | -------------- | ------------------- |
| Breakfast  | $20            | $30                 |
| Lunch      | $25            | $40                 |
| Dinner     | $45            | $65                 |
| **Total**  | **$90**        | **$135**            |

- Alcoholic beverages are **not reimbursable**.
- Client entertainment meals may exceed per diem limits with VP pre-approval and must include the names and titles of all attendees.

## 5. Mileage Reimbursement

Employees using their personal vehicle for business purposes will be reimbursed at the **CRA-prescribed rate** (currently **$0.70/km** for the first 5,000 km and **$0.64/km** thereafter).

## 6. Expense Submission

- All expense reports must be submitted within **15 business days** of the trip's completion.
- Original receipts (or clear digital scans) are required for any expense exceeding **$25**.
- Expense reports submitted more than 30 days late may be denied.
- Reimbursements are processed within **10 business days** of approval.

## 7. Non-Reimbursable Expenses

The following are **not reimbursable**:
- Personal entertainment (movies, minibar, spa)
- Traffic or parking violations
- Travel insurance (covered by corporate policy)
- Upgrades not approved in advance
- Expenses for companions or family members

## 8. Consequences of Policy Violations

- First violation: Written warning and mandatory policy review.
- Second violation: Reimbursement privileges suspended for 90 days; all expenses require pre-approval.
- Third violation: Disciplinary action up to and including termination.

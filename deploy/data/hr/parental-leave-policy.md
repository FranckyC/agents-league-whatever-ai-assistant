# Parental Leave Policy

**Policy ID:** HR-POL-004  
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2025  
**Last Revised:** July 5, 2025  
**Approved By:** David Chen, Chief People Officer

---

## 1. Purpose

This policy outlines the parental leave benefits available to employees of Contoso Ltd. following the birth, adoption, or foster placement of a child.

## 2. Eligibility

All full-time employees who have completed at least **12 months** of continuous employment are eligible for parental leave benefits. Part-time employees working 20+ hours per week are eligible for prorated benefits.

## 3. Leave Entitlement

| Leave Type                  | Duration           | Pay                          |
| --------------------------- | ------------------ | ---------------------------- |
| **Birth Parent Leave**      | Up to 18 weeks     | 100% salary for first 12 weeks; 75% for weeks 13–18 |
| **Non-Birth Parent Leave**  | Up to 8 weeks      | 100% salary                  |
| **Adoption / Foster Leave** | Up to 12 weeks     | 100% salary for first 8 weeks; 75% for weeks 9–12 |

### 3.1 Extended Unpaid Leave

After exhausting paid parental leave, employees may request an additional **unpaid leave of up to 26 weeks** under the federal Employment Insurance program. Job protection applies for the full duration.

## 4. Benefits During Leave

- Health, dental, and vision insurance continue during paid and unpaid parental leave at no additional cost to the employee.
- Pension contributions continue during paid leave only. During unpaid leave, the employee may elect to continue contributions at their own expense.
- Vacation days do not accrue during unpaid leave.

## 5. Requesting Parental Leave

- Employees must notify their manager and HR **at least 30 days** before the anticipated start date (or as soon as practicable in cases of emergency or early delivery).
- A **Parental Leave Request Form** must be submitted via the HR Portal with supporting documentation (e.g., medical certificate, adoption decree).

## 6. Return to Work

- Employees returning from parental leave are guaranteed the same or an equivalent position with the same salary and benefits.
- A **gradual return schedule** (e.g., 4-day workweek for up to 4 weeks) may be requested and will be considered based on business needs.

## 7. Parental Leave for Multiple Births / Adoptions

In the case of multiple births or simultaneous adoption of more than one child, an additional **4 weeks** of paid leave is granted.

## 8. Contact

For parental leave questions, contact the **HR Benefits Team** at **hr-benefits@contoso.com** or ext. **4500**.

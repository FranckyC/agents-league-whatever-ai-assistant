# Remote Work & Hybrid Arrangement Policy

**Policy ID:** HR-POL-003  
**Effective Date:** February 1, 2025  
**Last Revised:** August 20, 2025  
**Approved By:** Sarah Mitchell, VP of Human Resources

---

## 1. Purpose

This policy defines the guidelines and expectations for remote work and hybrid arrangements at Contoso Ltd.

## 2. Eligibility

Remote and hybrid work arrangements are available to employees whose role responsibilities can be effectively performed outside the office, as determined by their manager and HR. Eligibility criteria include:

- Minimum **6 months** of continuous employment at Contoso Ltd.
- A performance rating of "Meets Expectations" or higher in the most recent review cycle.
- No active disciplinary actions or Performance Improvement Plans.

## 3. Work Arrangement Types

| Arrangement   | Description                                                  |
| ------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **Fully Remote** | Employee works from home or an approved remote location 5 days per week. |
| **Hybrid**       | Employee works from the office a minimum of **2 days per week** (Tuesday and Thursday are designated in-office days). |
| **On-Site**      | Employee works from the office 5 days per week.              |

## 4. Requesting a Remote/Hybrid Arrangement

1. Submit a **Remote Work Request Form** via the HR Portal.
2. The employee's direct manager reviews and provides a recommendation within **5 business days**.
3. HR reviews and issues a final decision within **10 business days**.
4. Approved arrangements are reviewed every **6 months**.

## 5. Workspace Requirements

Employees working remotely must:

- Maintain a dedicated, distraction-free workspace.
- Have a reliable internet connection with a minimum speed of **50 Mbps download / 10 Mbps upload**.
- Use company-provided equipment (laptop, monitor, headset). A one-time **$500 home office stipend** is provided upon approval of a remote or hybrid arrangement.
- Comply with all IT security policies, including the use of the company VPN.

## 7. International Remote Work

Working from outside Canada requires prior written approval from HR and Legal due to tax and regulatory implications. Requests must be submitted at least **30 days** in advance and are limited to a maximum of **30 calendar days per year**.

## 8. Revocation

The company reserves the right to revoke remote or hybrid privileges with **15 business days** notice if business needs change, performance declines, or policy violations occur.
